• var(--font-primary)

Alegreya Sans is the primary typeface

The primary typeface represents a strong and bold face with lines that show stability and arrogance to fully express the power and dominance of our company.

Use the primary typeface for body copy and any captions.

Source Code Pro is the secondary typeface

The secondary typeface is a compressed serif that really shows strength and dominance over our domain.

The secondary typeface should be used for headings, buttons and to highlight important things for the consumer to be aware of.
